Data Deletion Options

We respect your right to control your personal data. You can request either complete account deletion or deletion of specific data types while keeping your account active.

delete_forever

Complete Account Deletion

  • Account permanently deleted
  • All data removed from servers
  • Cannot be recovered
  • Lose access to all features
cleaning_services

Partial Data Deletion

  • Keep your account active
  • Delete specific data types
  • Maintain access to features
  • Can continue using the service

Before You Delete

Consider these alternatives to permanent account deletion:

Account Deactivation

Temporarily deactivate your account instead of permanent deletion. You can reactivate later.

Data Export

Export your data before deletion to keep a personal copy of your links and collections.

Privacy Settings

Adjust your privacy settings to limit data collection while keeping your account active.
