Account & Data Deletion

We respect your right to have your personal data deleted. If you wish to permanently delete your Linkker account and all associated data, you can submit a request through this page.

What happens when you delete your account?

  • Your account will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered
  • All your saved links, collections, and preferences will be removed
  • Your personal information will be deleted from our servers
  • You will lose access to any premium features or subscriptions
  • Deletion is typically completed within 30 days of your request

Consider these alternatives to permanent account deletion:

Temporarily deactivate your account instead of permanent deletion. You can reactivate later.

Data Export

Export your data before deletion to keep a personal copy of your links and collections.

Adjust your privacy settings to limit data collection while keeping your account active.
